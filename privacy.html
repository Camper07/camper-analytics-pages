Camper Analytics - Privacy Policy

Camper Analytics is a private analytics tool used only by the owner.

The tool may read basic account information and public video statistics from the TikTok account that authorizes access, such as public video metadata, views, likes, comments, shares, and publish dates.

The data is stored only in the owner's private Google Sheet.

The tool does not sell data, share data with third parties, post content, edit videos, delete videos, hide videos, or access private messages.

The app is read-only and is used only for personal analytics and video performance tracking.

Access can be revoked at any time from TikTok account settings.
